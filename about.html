About


“From the Greek para, against or counter to, and doxa, opinion
or belief, a paradox is an idea that contradicts a more generally
accepted idea, on purpose.”
—The Paradox of Knowledge and Power, Keenan (1987)


Paradoxes are known to be arguments with invalid logic,
or revealed errors in definition, but they may encourage
more critical thinking and let people come up with some
special ideas in different areas such as math or physics.

You will be introduced some famous paradoxes here!
